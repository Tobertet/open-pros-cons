With Open Pros & Cons you can create all the arguments and pros and cons lists you need in order to make your decisions easier.

Don't worry about trackers, ads and malware because you won't find any in this app. 

Open Pros & Cons has been destributed using the GPL license and is totally free as in freedom and as in free beer.

Take a look at the code and make all the suggestions and countributions you want in the following URL https://github.com/Tobertet/open-pros-cons
